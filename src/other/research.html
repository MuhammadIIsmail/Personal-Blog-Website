Computational chemistry is a field of chemistry which uses computer simulations for studying the chemistry of matter.

It is used in drug discovery in a field called computational drug design. Drug design comprises many techniques according to its type:

  • Structure-Based Drug Design
    1. Molecular Docking
    2. Denovo design

  • Ligand-Based Drug Design
    1. Pharmacophore modeling
    2. QSAR
